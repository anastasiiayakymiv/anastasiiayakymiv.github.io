High quality

The five-star Bank Hotel was reopened to visitors in 2016. The building was renovated and modernized to meet the expectations of the most demanding guests. We offer luxurious rooms, numerous facilities, and exceptional service. The hotel is located near the city center, which makes it the best option for those who travel for business and travel purposes.
